ABOUT

Where Fitness Meets Community

OUR STORY

Welcome to Pump Zone, where we transform fitness goals into reality. Since 2023, we've been helping our members build strength, endurance, and confidence in our facility. We have 2 branches, Pump Zone - San Pedro & Pump Zone - San Francisco.

Open daily from 8 AM to 10 PM, our gym offers a full range of clean equipment, specialized training zones, and a vibrant, supportive community that keeps you motivated every step of the way.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to inspire and motivate every member to push beyond their limits by providing them well-managed facilities, expert guidance, and a supportive community atmosphere that transforms every workout into an opportunity for growth, achievement, and lasting success.

OUR RULES

The gym welcomes members of all genders and ages but recommends teens (13–17), for guidance and safety training. Adults have full access. Gender specific areas (ex. women-only sections) may be available for privacy, but general zones are mixed with strict anti-harassment policies. All members must wear proper athletic attire, wipe down equipment, rerack weights, and avoid excessive noise. Dropping weights, hogging equipments, or unsafe behavior is prohibited. Hygiene is mandatory, bring your own towel. Personal trainers are recommended for beginners.
